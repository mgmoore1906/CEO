For Employers & Organizations

Partner With CEO

CEO employer partners gain access to a curated, pre-vetted pipeline of driven students who have been trained not just in skills, but in how to navigate professional environments and create value from day one.

Partnerships range from hosting fellows and providing site visits to funding specific cohorts or sector tracks. We work with organizations of all sizes.

01
Talent pipeline access — direct connection to emerging talent from communities often overlooked by traditional recruiting channels.
02
Brand visibility — recognition as a CEO partner across program materials, events, and digital channels reaching students, families, and funders.
03
Curriculum input — partners help shape what economic literacy means in their sector, creating students who understand your industry from the start.
04
CSR and DEI alignment — demonstrate meaningful commitment to economic equity and workforce development in your community.
