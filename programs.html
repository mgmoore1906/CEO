What We Offer

Programs Built for
Real Outcomes

Three interconnected pathways designed to move students from awareness to action to economic agency — at every stage of their journey.

Our Approach

Not Mentoring. Not Tutoring. Economic Architecture.

Every CEO program is grounded in one core belief: students don't just need skills — they need to understand the labor-market systems they're entering. We teach economic navigation alongside applied experience.

Each pathway connects directly to employer partners, sector opportunities, and a growing alumni network that students can leverage for life.

Core Pathways
Track 01

Economic Navigation

Most students graduate without understanding how labor markets work, how credentials translate into earnings, or how networks shape career trajectories. We fix that — before they step into the workforce.

Our Economic Navigation curriculum is a structured, multi-week intensive that teaches labor-market mechanics, salary negotiation, career pathway mapping, and the unwritten rules of professional advancement.

Labor market literacy & salary benchmarking
Career pathway mapping across high-value sectors
Credential navigation & return-on-investment analysis
Network architecture and professional relationship building

Track 02

Employer Fellowship

Our flagship intensive: a structured, cohort-based fellowship that places students directly inside partner companies for real work experience — not shadowing, not observation, but actual contribution with coaching and economic literacy woven throughout.

Fellows complete 800+ hours over the program cycle, work alongside professionals in their target sector, and graduate with a portfolio, a network, and a clear economic roadmap.

800+ hours of real work inside employer-partner organizations
1:1 coaching alongside economic literacy curriculum
Portfolio development and professional presentation training
Direct pipeline to full-time employment or further education

Track 03

Sector Accelerators

Deep-dive, sector-specific programs that give students concentrated exposure to high-growth industries with the highest return potential — including finance, technology, healthcare, and skilled trades.

Each accelerator is built in partnership with industry leaders and includes site visits, executive panels, applied projects, and a direct connection to sector-specific opportunity pipelines.

Finance, Technology, Healthcare & Skilled Trades tracks
Executive panels and site visits with sector leaders
Applied capstone projects reviewed by industry partners
Sector-specific credential and certification pathways

How to Join

The Application Process

Our process is designed to be accessible, transparent, and merit-based — focused on potential and drive, not just prior achievement.

01
Submit Application
Complete our online application including a short essay on your economic goals and the barriers you've faced.
02
Interview
Selected applicants are invited for a 30-minute conversation with the CEO team to discuss their aspirations and fit.
03
Selection
Cohort members are selected based on drive, economic need, and potential for impact within and beyond the program.
04
Onboarding
New fellows complete a two-day orientation covering economic literacy foundations before beginning their track.
Who Can Apply

Eligibility Requirements

We serve students who have the talent and drive but not always the access.

Who We Serve

  • High school students (9th–12th grade) or recent graduates
  • First-generation college students or career explorers
  • Students from households earning below area median income
  • Students in under-resourced schools or communities
  • Motivated learners with clear aspirations, regardless of GPA

What We Look For

  • Demonstrated curiosity and drive to grow
  • Willingness to engage fully in an intensive experience
  • Openness to feedback and new frameworks
  • Commitment to giving back to the CEO community
  • Clear, articulable economic or career goals
