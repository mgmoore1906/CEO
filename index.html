Preparing students for real economic opportunity.

Cohort for Economic Opportunity (CEO) helps high school students build industry-relevant skills, entrepreneurial confidence, workforce readiness, and social-emotional strength through a sustained four-year cohort model.

Explore the Model

Industry-Based Certifications

Students gain access to workforce-aligned certifications that validate skills and strengthen their readiness for future education and employment.

Entrepreneurship

CEO develops creative thinking, opportunity recognition, and practical problem-solving so students can build, lead, and innovate.

Workforce Readiness

Students build durable professional skills including communication, professionalism, teamwork, and career navigation.

Social-Emotional Learning

CEO supports confidence, resilience, self-awareness, and long-term personal growth so students can persist in competitive environments.

The gap between school and opportunity is still too wide.

Too many students move through high school without clear exposure to careers, verified skill development, entrepreneurial experience, or a structured path toward economic mobility. CEO exists to help close that gap.

Summer Intensive Experience

CEO launches each cohort through immersive summer programming designed to expose students to workforce pathways, certifications, leadership, and entrepreneurship.

School-Year Cohort Support

During the academic year, students participate in structured sessions that reinforce skill-building, reflection, professional development, and future planning.

Employer and Community Exposure

CEO connects students to professionals, employers, and community partners through site visits, guest speakers, mentorship, and real-world exposure.

Career and Postsecondary Navigation

Students gain clearer direction as they evaluate career interests, education options, industry pathways, and the steps required to compete in a changing labor market.

CEO is built for the full education-to-workforce ecosystem.

Our model is designed to support students while creating real value for school districts, families, employers, and mission-aligned funders.

School Districts

CEO complements existing district efforts with structured, partnership-ready, supplemental programming that extends student opportunity.

Employers

Employers gain a stronger way to engage early with future talent while helping shape awareness, readiness, and workforce exposure.

Funders

CEO provides a practical model for investing in workforce mobility, student development, and longer-term community impact.

Families & Students

Students and families gain a clearer pathway to confidence, exposure, readiness, and meaningful future opportunity.

Help build a stronger bridge from education to economic mobility.
