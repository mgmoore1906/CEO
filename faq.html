Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Is CEO a school or replacement for CTE classes?

No. CEO is supplemental programming that strengthens CTE pathways through cohort support, certification acceleration, and real-world exposure.

Is the well-being support therapy?

No. It is structured resilience and regulation skill-building (with clear boundaries, consent, and referral pathways as needed).

Is CEO an edtech platform?

The platform is enabling infrastructure — tracking certifications, skills, and portfolios — while human coaching drives outcomes.

Where does CEO operate?

CEO launches in Houston, Texas, with a model designed for replication through aligned partnerships.
